How to hire the ideal candidate

The first step to finding the ideal candidate for any position is to define the role of the position, then establish the wanted and needed skills for said position

Wants
These are the skills that are not required to perform the task at hand, but would help team production and could possibly help grow the position in the future

Needs
These are the skills that are used on a day to day basis, and it would be difficult or too time demanding to learn on the job
