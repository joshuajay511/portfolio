Best East Coast Winter Beach Vacation Spots
Many east coast states experience four full seasons, each offering its own unique weather patterns and conditions. East coast states are also home to some of the most stunning beaches that people will visit year-round. If you’re looking to plan a winter retreat in 2020, here are some of the best east coast winter beach vacation spots for you to consider.
 
Camden, Main

When you’re not enjoying a peaceful, snowy scene on the shores of the great state of Maine, shred down one of the 20 ski trails at the Jack Williams Toboggan Shoot while taking in the ocean views. You can also explore Camden’s lively downtown area with plenty of bars, shops, and restaurants for unbeatable cuisine.

Newport, Rhode Island

During the winter months, Newport becomes a tranquil and sleepy town. But that doesn’t mean there is nothing to do! Newport plays host to a number of fun events including a week of live music, a mac and cheese and chili cook-off during the Newport Winter Festival, seal-watch cruises, and polo right on Easton’s Beach.

Cape May, New Jersey

Cape May is well known for its National Historic District, home to almost 600 preserved Victorian buildings, where you can visit and tour. You can climb to the top of the Cape May Lighthouse to take in the surrounding views, and warm up with local wine and fresh-caught New England Lobster at the Cape May Winery and Vineyard.

Read More
Nantucket, Massachusetts

After strolling the crowd-free beaches, enjoy the off-season prices at many local restaurants including the Breeze Bar and Cafe and The SeaGrille Restaurant. Be sure to also visit Bartlett’s Farm to pick up some local produce or check out a lecture or other event. Top the day off by grabbing a local craft beer at the popular Cisco Brewery.
